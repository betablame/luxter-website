Lighting Control can transform any space to provide the best combination of natural and artificial light. Through decent design and using control solutions like automated shades and dimmable fixtures, you can accent your environment to suit any task or mood. 

LIGHTING CONTROL

Whether you are looking to update a single room to say a Home Cinema or are re-wiring an entire house,we offer the latest lighting control solutions from Lutron, Rako, Dynalite and Mode Lighting, including the entire range of Sivoia window treatments. 

LIGHTING DESIGN

We offer a complete design service for all of your lighting needs. It may be the case that you are trying to be more evironmentally and economically efficient, or you just want to completely change the appearance of a particular space.

GARDEN LIGHTING

A garden is an extension of your home and you may be looking to make more use of it as an entertaining space or just make it look visually stunning. We have remote control retrofit solutions that can plug into your existing wiring.

DYNAMIC ART

Want your house to continually evolve and change to suit your mood or the season. Ask us about our Dynamic Art installations utilizing projectors and TVs, for filling your home with interactive content.

Our Services

Design

After appraising your specific requirements we will advise and quote according to your needs and budget, designing a system to suit whether a single room or an entire house and garden installation.

Installation

Following the system design we will then liase with any other associated contractors and work to the project schedule and budget, installing all the required equipment to the highest of standards.

Programming

Whether you have an existing lighting control system or are having a new one installed we can help tailor either to your specific requirements including timed events, parental control and special occasions

Integration

All our systems are designed with integration in mind, whether that is for smart phones, tablets, security and cctv. Whatever you need we can give you the control you need wherever and whenever.
